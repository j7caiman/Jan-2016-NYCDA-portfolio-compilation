About the Class

Art and the Fundamentals of Programming

Learn to program in JavaScript by creating procedurally generated art. This class is designed to be challenging, allowing students the opportunity to overcome intense challenges, experience the joy of discovery and problem solving, and instill a lifelong appreciation for artistic creation. The class will meet once per week for two hours at a time and will include assignments outside of class. Come prepared to work hard. No prior programming experience necessary. All age groups welcome.

Meet the Staff

Jon Miller is a native of Pittsburgh & earned a BS in Computer & Electrical Engineering in 2010. After working as a successful software engineer with Qualcomm in San Diego & Flurry in NYC, Jon has decided to focus completely on his passion & gifting as a teacher. Through networking connections, Jon gravitated to NC after teaching for NY Code & Design in Philadelphia, Amsterdam, & Durham. After creating and teaching several classes in web development for adults, Jon feels ready to inspire and galvanize the next generation of students. Jon is currently focused on his vision to teach coding to youths as a pathway for skill development & character growth because Jon believes that well-taught coding encourages the student to become a better person.
